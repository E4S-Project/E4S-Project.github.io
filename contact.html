E4S represents a growing community of HPC software products. Please contact us if you would like your product to be a part of E4S.

Get Support

E4S provides a light-weight layer on top of hundreds of open source software packages, driven by the Spack package management system.

For support, you may refer to the following:

  1. General support guide: E4S Readthedocs Support Guide
  2. Issues with E4S top layer: Support for E4S itself can be obtained by filing an issue at the E4S-Project GitHub site.
  3. Issues with E4S primary packages: The packages listed on the E4S Product Information page have contact and support information at the associated link for each product.

Questions?

General questions about E4S can be directed to the E4S Project Lead, Michael Heroux, mheroux@acm.org

Technical questions about E4S can be directed to the E4S Technical Lead, Sameer Shende, sameer@cs.uoregon.edu
